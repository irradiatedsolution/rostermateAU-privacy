Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 April 2026

Irradiated Solution built RosterMateAU as a commercial app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

RosterMateAU does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data entered into the app — including profiles, roster entries, shift times, and pay information — is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any external server.

2. In-App Purchases

RosterMateAU offers optional In-App Purchases (Monthly subscription and Lifetime purchase) processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services within the app. No data is shared with any third parties.

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

5. Children's Privacy

RosterMateAU is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
